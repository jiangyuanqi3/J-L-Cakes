Welcome to the cake of your dreams!

At J&L Cakes, we design and create Wedding Cakes, Celebration Cakes, Corporate Cakes and cakes of all shapes and sizes, customised to your specific tastes.

In fact, we can create the cake of your dreams that reflects your personal style!

To see some of the many beautiful cakes that we have created in the years that we have been in business, you can browse a category from the options on the top.

Hopefully, looking through our samples will inspire you... and provide you with ideas about what is possible!

We also stock party accessories such as napkins, plates, cups, partybags, confetti etc and wedding items from cake knives to ring cushions.

We have a vast choice of Cake Decorating supplies for all your cake decorating needs including, cake boards, cake boxes, icing, coloured icing, cup cake cases and boxs, ribbon and much much more!
